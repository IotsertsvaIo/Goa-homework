ABOUT MARVEL COMICS

Marvel Comics is an American comic book and related media publishing company. It is owned by Marvel Publishing and is a branch of Marvel Entertainment. Marvel's heroes include such famous heroes as Captain America, Fantastic Four, Hulk, Iron Man, Spider-Man, X-Men, Thor (Heimdall, Odin, Loki, Frigga, Sif and the three minions) and others. Most of these heroes exist in one reality called the Marvel Universe. Comic book publishing began in 1939 under the name Timely Comics, and by 1950 it was called Atlas Comics. Since the creation of Marvel in 1961, the creators of the Fantastic Four and many other superheroes have been: Stan Lee, Jack Kirby, Steve Ditko and others. It was then that Marvel became one of the largest comic book companies in America, along with DC Comics.
